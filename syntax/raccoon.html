BCs

  • raccoon App
  • LoadingUnloadingDirichletBCapplies a loading/unloading BC. The load ramps up linearly until it reaches the load cap. Once the load cap is reached, the load cap is incremented and unlading begins. Once the unloaded indicator becomes negative, loading starts. If the load cap exceeds the ultimate load, the entire loading/unloading terminates, and the current simulation is terminated.
  • ModeISurfingDirichletBCapplies the Dirichlet BC conforming with the analytical solution of a Mode-I crack. The crack is assumed to be emanating from the origin.
