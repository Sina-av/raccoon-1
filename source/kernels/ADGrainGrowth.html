ADGrainGrowth

Grain-Boundary model poly-crystalline interface Allen-Cahn Kernel

ADGrainGrowth is the automatic differentiation version of the ACGrGrPoly Kernel and can be activated in the GrainGrowthAction through the use_automatic_differentiation parameter.

The Kernel implements the bulk part of the grain growth model Moelans et al. (2008) Allen-Cahn equation where is the mobility, the kernel variable, and are the other order parameters. and are model parameters contributing to the grain boundary energy as where is the gradient energy coefficient from ADACInterface and is a function that needs to be numerically determined (see Moelans et al. (2008) for details).
