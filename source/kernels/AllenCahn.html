AllenCahn

Allen-Cahn Kernel that uses a DerivativeMaterial Free Energy

where is the variable the kernel is acting on, (mob_name) its associated mobility, and (f_name) is the bulk free energy density of the system which is provided by a function material. (args) are additional variable dependencies of the mobility and free energy density.

The dependent terms in the free energy functional of the system that arise from the gradient interface energy are handled separately in the ACInterface kernel.

    Description:Base name of the free energy function F defined in a DerivativeParsedMaterial
