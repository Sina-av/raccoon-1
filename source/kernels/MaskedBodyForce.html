MaskedBodyForce

Kernel that defines a body force modified by a material mask

Implements a kernel for a source term/body force limited to a certain region by a mask (material property). A function or a postprocessor can also be supplied to multiply the source term by. Contributions added by this kernel have the form where is the mask (supplied as a material property), is the constant source term/body force, is a function (optional), and is the value of a postprocessor (optional).
