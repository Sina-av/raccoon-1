ADHeatConduction

Description

ADHeatConduction is the implementation of the heat diffusion equation in HeatConduction within the framework of automatic differentiation. The ADHeatConduction kernel implements the heat equation given by Fourier's Law where The heat flux is given as where denotes the thermal conductivity of the material. can either be an ADMaterial or traditional Material.

This class inherits from the ADDiffusion class.

Same as Diffusion in terms of physics/residual, but the Jacobian is computed using forward automatic differentiation
