Gradient energy Allen-Cahn Kernel

Implements the Allen-Cahn term for the gradient energy contribution for the isotropic mobility case. Its weak form is

where (kappa_name) is the gradient energy coefficient, the non-conserved non-linear order parameter variable the kernel is acting on, (mob_name) is the scalar (isotropic) mobility associated with the order parameter, and is the test function.
