MathEBFreeEnergy

Material that implements the math free energy using the expression builder and automatic differentiation

Implements the bulk free free energy density

using the ExpressionBuilder system, where (c) is a coupled concentration. The minima of this free energy density lie at and . See also SplitCHMath.

Input Parameters

  • cConcentration variable

    C++ Type:std::vector<VariableName>

    Options:

    Description:Concentration variable

    Options:

    Options:

    Description:When false, MOOSE will not call compute methods on this material. The user must call computeProperties() after retrieving the MaterialBase via MaterialBasePropertyInterface::getMaterialBase(). Non-computed MaterialBases are not sorted for dependencies.
