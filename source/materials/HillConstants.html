Hill Constants

Build and rotate the Hill Tensor. It can be used with other Hill plasticity and creep materials.

Description

HillConstants material transforms the user specified hill constants from global co-ordinates to the material co-ordinates and stores them as a material property. It rotates the hill constants during the initial time step only. This class does not rotate the hill tensor during the simulation.
